Lasagna

Description

Lasagna with Meat Sauce is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, this lasagna recipe might seem difficult at first with expert cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

Step 1 Cook the pork and mutton meat
In a skillet, cook mutton and sausage over medium heat until they are soft and tender. You must keep the flame low and the pan covered so that the meats are properly cooked. You may need to add small quantities of water if you find the meats to be too dry during the process.
Step 2 The meat sauce
Add tomato sauce, tomato puree, garlic cloves, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano in the skillet. Simmer the ingredients, uncovered, for about an hour, stirring occasionally. Keep the flame low or the liquids will all dry up. Your meat sauce is ready!
